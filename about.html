About Me

What is there to know about Peter?

My name is Peter Tchuisse, I am a first-generation college student attending Indiana University Bloomington majoring in Informatics with a cognate in Business and minoring in Healthcare Management and Policy. I am apart of the Groups Scholars program and many on campus organizations such as African Student Association, Supply Chain Management and Operations Association, and the National Society of Black Engineers.

I have had experience working on campus roles that focus on operations, communcations, and helping others. I used to work at Indiana University's Recreational Sports Center as an Operations Assistant. As an operations assistant, I was tasked with making sure all machines within the facility were working properly. As well as making sure they are cleaned regularly. Along with this, I was tasked with helping anyone who comes into the gym find their way around. This job greatly helped me develop my social skills and professional attitude.

I currently working at Student Central, Office of the Bursar as a Part-Time Student Services Advisor. Here, I am tasked with looking into financial aid information and datasheets. I am required to talk to parents and student's alike to help them answer any financial aid questions they may have. In this job, I work with a system called the Student Information System (SIS). This system shows the data of students enrolled across all IU campuses. It is a complex database that shows the classes a student is taking, as well as the financial aid information. This job is improving my adaptabilty skills, as well as further developing my social skills. I am determined to continue learning, growing, and devloping my professional career.
