Student experience

ConductConnect

In my I300 (Human Interaction and Design) class, me and a group of 5 were tasked with coming up with a new idea IU could implement. We as a group came up with ConductConnect. ConductConnect is an app that can help students report, or get help on any type of misconduct that happens here at Indiana University Bloomington. Using Figma, we created ConductConnect and receieved 2nd place in an in class competition! Here is the link to our Conduct Connect project if you would like to view it!

Role: Research · Flows · Wireframes

Capstone Pitch

During my Freshman year of college, I took BUS-C 104 (Business Presenations) and for my final project I was tasked with making a new business idea that people could utilize. I came up with the battery transfer app. This app can share power to others in need of power. It tracks the devices battery health, battery Management, and also has a function called emergency energy in case of any dire situations. If you're Interested in hearing more, here is the link to my Capstone Pitch!

Aldi Online

During my freshman year, I took a class called K201-The Computer in Business. During this class, I was tasked with working with a team to try and discover new ways Aldi can innovate their business admist the COVID-19 pandemic. Me and my group worked together and came up with Aldi Online. A place where shoppers can buy their groceries freely without worry of being in contact with others!
